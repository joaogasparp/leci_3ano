PARA SEREM INDEPENDENTES -> P(A ∩ B) = P(A) * P(B)

EVENTO A:

(3, 6), (4, 5), (5, 4) e (6, 3)

P(A) = 4/36 = 1/9

____________________________________________________________________________

EVENTO B:

(2, 4 e 6)

P(B) = 3/6 = 1/2

____________________________________________________________________________

PROBABILIDADE CONJUNTA:

P(A) * P(B) = (1/9) * (1/2) = 1/18
